    About Hairapy


    The concept behind Hairapy emerged from discussions within our team about the challenges in finding suitable hair products for our individual hair concerns. Given that everyone's hair is unique, it can be difficult to identify the best products to address specific hair issues effectively.

    Recognizing the difficulty in finding appropriate hair products, we formulated a problem statement:


    "Can we create a solution that helps people find the most suitable Indonesian hair products?"



    Considering the time and resources required, the time and resources required, we decided to start with a small scope. Initially, we aim to develop a product that is effective for our team. Once we achieve this, we can then expand our efforts to create a broader solution.
